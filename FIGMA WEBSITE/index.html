Our Story

At FreshNest Cleaning Co., our journey began with one mop, one bucket, and one big goal — to make everyday spaces feel extraordinary. Since 2021, we’ve built our reputation on care, reliability, and the touch of shine in every corner we clean.

We know that tidy spaces create more than clean floors. It means peace of mind, pride, and the comfort of being surrounded by calm energy. That’s why every sweep, scrub, and polish comes from the heart — because we believe homes should feel like fresh starts.
