How to Choose a Crystal

  • Start with your intention (love, calm, protection, focus).
  • Notice which shapes you like holding — palm stones vs. towers.
  • Cleanse after purchasing (water-safe options only!).

Cleansing Basics

  • Moonlight: set on a windowsill overnight.
  • Selenite: rest other crystals near a selenite wand.
  • Smoke: waft incense; avoid direct ash on stones.

Crystal Quick Guide

  • Amethyst: calm + intuition
  • Rose Quartz: love + compassion
  • Obsidian: protection + grounding
  • Citrine: joy + abundance
  • Angelite: soothing + communication
  • Selenite: cleansing + clarity
